Smart Agro System as the name suggests solves your agricultural problems using the technology of “Internet Of Things(IoT) and Machine learning ".SAS contains Multiple Features. Irrigation System, Mineral detection System and The Pest Management System.

A solar powered Water pump is used to supply water to the fields whose activity will be governed by the farmer from any part of the globe.The values of water content and temperature are tracked lively and sent to farmer’s mobile phone through an app and maintained in a database. Based on the notification sent regarding the humidity of the soil the farmer can switch on/off the motor and control the irrigation pattern of his land.When the water level is critically low the motor turn on automatically.
